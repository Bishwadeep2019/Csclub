What we do?

Welcome to computer science club, we are happy to see you here, this is a place where you learn to code and reimagine technology join us and we will go on a journey of lifetime friendship, in this journey we will learn various things not only coding but how to manage events. Where technology meets creativity

How we work?

You must be curious to know how to get into the club and how this club function, so we are here to guide you. You can join the club by reaching out to the prime commitee members or reach us on our club's instagram profile and we will join you in. So as you progress in the club, participate in events and organise few events you will be given few roles in the club.Our main events are WebJam, Python workshop, Blind Coding.
